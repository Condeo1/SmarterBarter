FAQ

What is Smarter Barter?

Smarter Barter provides a platform for people to offer a service or services, such as catering or gardening,
in exchange for another service or services, such as music lessions or mechanic work. Smarter Barter will
attempt to match people as best as possible, and may create chains of people providing services in order
to satisfy requests.

What does it cost?

Our website itself doesn't cost anything to use, although the service you provide to someone may cost you money
depending on the cost of materials for your service.

How do I use it?

After signing up with our website and posting the services you are able to provide, search for a service
you require. The system will attempt to match you with another user who lives in the same zip code who is able
to provide the service you need in exchange for a service you can provide.

In some cases, you may be paired with someone who needs your service but cannot provide the service you need.
In those cases, the person you are paired with will be matched with a person who needs the service your partner
provides, and if the third person provides the service you need, you will be matched with them as will. This chain
can potentially continue for several people before it wraps back around to you.
